Summary

Set in a rustic village in East Godavari (fictional Devipuram), the story centers on childhood friends Gajapathi Varma (Manchu Manoj), Varadha (Nara Rohith), and Seenu (Bellamkonda Sai Sreenivas)—an orphan raised by the community. Their loyalty is shattered when a corrupt minister sets his sights on acquiring the 75‑acre Varahi Amma temple land (worth ₹1,000 crore) by sowing distrust and exploiting vulnerabilities. The narrative unfolds as trust breaks, alliances shift, and the fight for justice turns personal
